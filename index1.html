Engross Yourself In the field of Aerospace

Aerospace Engineering deals with the research, design, development, construction, testing, collaboration processes, science and technology of aircraft & spacecraft, and their products. It involves the study of science and engineering behind the physical properties of rockets, aircrafts and spacecrafts.

Categories

HARDWARE

The Hardware wing of Aerospace Club mainly focuses on the Communication, Power Supply, Navigation, Fuel Conservation, Safety Against Vibration and Shock, Structural integrity of the the planes and other devices.

SOFTWARE

The Software wing of Aerospace Club mainly focuses on using simulation and computer programs and software to calculate and design the desired model for construction.

DESIGNING

The Design wing of Aerospace Club mainly focuses on making the design of the aircraft and other concerned machines. It also deals with the promotion and social media aspect of the clubs.

MANUFACTURING

The Manufacturing wing of Aerospace Club mainly focuses on choosing materials and making the simulated models in real life i.e. it basically deals with producing the desired result in product form.
